Our Programmes

Personal development is at the heart of 5Girls Project approach, it will involve the entire world of each of our girls, recognizing and developing their knowledge, attitudes, skills, relationships, and behavior that can be utilized in and outside of the classroom. This involves thinking processes, managing emotions, values, and relationships along with a range of life skills that will assist the girls in coping with the challenges of everyday lives.

Our Programmes take a holistic approach, which has been tailored, to develop girls and young women’s strengths, interests, goals, dreams and challenge them beyond what they believe they can do. We provide them the opportunity to find their voices and recognize their strengths and utilize this to create a future they can be proud of.

Girls are required to create a personal development plan, identifying areas of their lives they need to address and where they want to progress. The girls then make action points and are supported to move forward positively in different areas of their lives.

Elevating Girls Vacation Programme

Queens Training

Aims to educate and train girls to build up their advocacy, leadership, and confidence skills to be able to be peer educators to challenge and influence attitudinal change towards gender based violence and other issues affecting girls and women in Ghana. Girl Talk

Provides girls mentorship and coaching from positive female role models, aimed to empower and raise the girl’s aspirations and ambition. Speakers also share how they can turn their academic interest, hobbies, and passions into a sustainable business. We believe that when girls realize what is possible, they are more likely to find inspiration and become active agents of change. IT Skill Training

Provides girls with intense practical IT lessons to ensure that they've the skills for success beyond the classroom. We believe that we have an important role to play in promoting equal opportunities and challenging gender-biased career and life aspirations to reduce the gender gap in pay and opportunities !.The lessons take place straight after the girls have their theory ICT lesson.This enables them to put their newly acquired knowledge straight into practice.
