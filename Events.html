"Queens in Training programme goes NATIONAL"(Workshops in Volta Region)

It gives us great pleasure to announce that we have expanded this programme by offering free,creative and interactive group prevention workshops across different regions in Ghana. We are on a huge mission to ensure that girls and young women in all areas across Ghana do not continue to suffer and experience gender based violence in silence!.Evidently, we traveled to the spectacular Volta Region of Ghana were we delivered a few workshops addressing the issue of gender based violence. We met some phenomenal young queens , who showcased their creative talents. They produced amazing drama performances and art work to illustrate the effects and impact of gender based violence.

"Girls CAN do Tech Too"

In a world that is constantly changing, we believe that it is essential that we provide our girls with the practical skills and experience relevant to the changing world. With this in mind, we added practical IT skills training lessons to our existing programmes which commenced in January 2018. We are happy to announce that our girls have shown great interest and commitment to improving their IT knowledge. The girls attend our practical IT skills training sessions which take place straight after their ICT theory lesson, enabling them to put into practice their newly acquired knowledge . Their enthusiasm, determination and drive to complete set tasks makes us confident in their ability contribute and benefit to the changing world.
