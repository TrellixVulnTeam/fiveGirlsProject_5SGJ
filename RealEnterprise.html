Real Cosmetics

The agriculture sector dominates the Ghanaian economy. However, the farming population is aging as youth migrate to urban areas, thus there is a real need for young people to be empowered to bring energy, vitality & innovation into this sector. This motivated us to create an enterprise (REAL) that utilises Ghana's natural resources and in turn enables us to re-invest the profits into the education and training of girls across Africa. Through REAL we provide quality coconut cosmetics and well-being products , ethically sourced from Ghana!
