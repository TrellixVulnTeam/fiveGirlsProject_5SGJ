Welcome

5Girls Project invests in the education, skills training and self-confidence of girls and women in Ghana. We believe that if given their rights to reach the full potential, girls and women have the power to elevate Africa!

Our Values: Responsibilty Collaberation&Partners Accountability Child Participation

Through our three core programmes we have been promoting gender equality and the empowerment of girls in Junior high school education. We are currently, working towards extending our services to young mothers who are not in any form of education, training, or employment (NEET).

5GP Specifically Aim To:

Increase the number of girls transitioning from Junior High to Senior High School Education. Increase access to information, training and employment opportunities for girls and women. Empower girls and women to directly challenge attitudes around normalization of gender-based violence. Offer a safe space for girls and women to meet, socialize and discuss the issues which are of importance to them. Also, developing their confidence, resilience, raise their self-esteem and leadership skills.

Our Vision

To see the rights of girls and women being fully acknowledged, enablingthem to reach their full potential and in turn contribute significantly to Ghana's economic and social development.
